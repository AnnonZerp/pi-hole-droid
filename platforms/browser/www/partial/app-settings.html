settings

Here you can manage all the profiles of the app.

You can have multiple profiles to manage different Pi-holes in different places (e.g. local Pi-holes or remotes Pi-holes).

For example: you can create a "local" profile (http://10.20.30.46) to manage your Pi-hole from home and another profile named "remote" (http://my-remote-host.ext) to manage your Pi-hole when you are not connected to your local network.
